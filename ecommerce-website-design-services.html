eCommerce Web Development Platforms:

Magento Development

Magento is one of the widely used development platforms today when it comes to eCommerce website development. Due to its modular structure, it allows deep customization of both front-end and back-end. It is effective in building an online marketplace where multiple vendor stores can be set up, and providing an unmatched level of security due to its PA-DSS compliance.

OpenCart Development

One of the main perks of OpenCart is easy customization. Thanks to the use of AJAX, websites developed using OpenCart are extremely lightweight. It supports over 14000 additional extensions which can be downloaded, including low cost premium plug-ins. Due to a lot of experience as an eCommerce web development company in India, our professionals can deliver beautiful OpenCart based online stores for any industry.

PrestaShop Development

When using PrestaShop, you do not have to deal with a lot of technical aspects or complex coding. It is free, lightweight and easy to use. Besides, it offers multilingual support which allows you to sell your products or services in a global market. Our professional eCommerce website developers are well acquainted with this platform and can build unique eCommerce websites for your business.

WordPress Development

WordPress websites are the fastest and easiest to develop, yet turn out to be extremely robust. By using a few among the many amazing eCommerce plug-ins available for WordPress, excellent eCommerce solutions for small businesses can be created quickly. We generate beautiful websites using WooCommerce, which drives about 30% of all eCommerce websites – more than any other eCommerce development platform.

Custom PHP Development

To help implement your specific requirements for larger and complex eCommerce solutions such as an online marketplace, custom PHP is the way to go. Hire our PHP developers to get feature rich, modern and lightweight eCommerce websites with exclusive designs for your business. We use frameworks like Laravel, CakePHP, CodeIgniter and databases like MySQL, MS-SQL, PostgreSQL, etc. to deliver robust and scalable eCommerce online stores.

Shopify Development

One of the most accessible platforms in eCommerce website development, Shopify offers the advantage of being able to get off the block and set up an online store for your business quickly. You can build eCommerce stores with Shopify in an uncomplicated manner to sell your products online. The interface is easy to use, customizable according to your needs and has inbuilt security as well.
